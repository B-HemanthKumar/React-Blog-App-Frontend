Pathaan is a 2023 Indian Hindi-language action thriller film written and directed by Siddharth Anand and produced by Aditya Chopra under his banner Yash Raj Films.[1] The film stars Shah Rukh Khan, John Abraham and Deepika Padukone.[1] It is the fourth installment in the YRF Spy Universe after Ek Tha Tiger (2012), Tiger Zinda Hai (2017) and War (2019).

The film is scheduled to release on 25 January 2023, coinciding with the Indian Republic Day weekend.[2]

Production

Yash Raj Films, Shah Rukh Khan, Deepika Padukone and John Abraham officially announced Pathaan on 2 March 2022 at their social media accounts, revealing the release date to be 25 January 2023 with a first look teaser.[3] However, Various cast and crew of the film including Salman Khan and Vishal Dadlani have unofficially announced the film at various occasions before.[citation needed] Dadlani unofficially announced the film on his Twitter account. He wrote in the tweet, "No number from the past matters, no number in the future is too big. The whole world is waiting to watch Shahrukh. More importantly, we're all working towards a kickass film with great songs."[4] This film is YRF's first Dolby Cinema release and the first Bollywood film to be shot fully with IMAX cameras. This will be the first universe entry, in Indian cinema, to feature two post-credit scenes, akin to the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

Casting

Khan was cast in the film on September 2020.[5] Padukone joined the filming on 23 November 2020 and[6][7][8] Abraham joined the casting of the film in June 2021 during the shooting at Mumbai.[9][10] Dimple Kapadia was cast in December 2020.[11][12] Salman Khan was cast to make a cameo appearance on November 2020, reprising his role as Avinash Singh Rathore, from the Tiger franchise.[13][14] Hrithik Roshan was also cast to make a cameo in January 2022, reprising his role as Major Kabir Dhaliwal, from War.

Filming

Principal photography of the film began on 17 November 2020 in Mumbai.[15][16] In January 2021, filming moved to Dubai. Some of the major action sequences including a long chase sequence were shot in the same schedule.[17][18] On 12 April 2021, after some crew members of the film tested positive for COVID-19, further schedules of the film were postponed.[19] The filming resumed on 25 June 2021.[20][21][22] Later, in July 2021, another schedule of the film began.[23] A song was scheduled to be shot from 7 October 2021 in Mallorca and Cádiz[24][25] but the schedule was postponed.[26]

In February 2022, the production moved to Spain to shoot action sequences, in which, Khan, Padukone, and Abraham took part in.[27] Filming was set to take place in Mallorca and Cádiz, in south-western Spain.[28] The Spain schedule was completed in March 2022. Only the Mumbai schedule was left, which the unit completed in Yash Raj Studios, before calling it a complete wrap.

Music

The music was composed by Vishal–Shekhar, marking their seventh collaboration with Siddharth Anand, after Salaam Namaste, Tara Rum Pum, Bachna Ae Haseeno, Anjaana Anjaani, Bang Bang!, and War.[29]

Vishal-Shekhar roped in music producer, Meghdeep Bose, from Tiger Zinda Hai, for the purpose of music arrangement and production.[30] The background score of the film was composed by the duo, Sanchit Balhara and Ankit Balhara, marking their collaboration with Anand, after War.

Marketing

The teaser of the film was released on 2 November 2022, coinciding with Shah Rukh Khan's 57th birthday.[31]

Release

After filming for over 1.5 years, Pathaan is scheduled for a theatrical release on 25 January 2023, coinciding with Republic day in standard and IMAX versions.[32]
